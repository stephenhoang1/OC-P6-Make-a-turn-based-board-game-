Game Rules

Tempers boil over between two cooks in this turn-based 2 player battle game! Which one will emerge as the head chef?

Movements

  • If your player panel is green, it's your turn.
  • Run your mouse curser over your player to see possible moves.
  • Click on a red highlighted square to move there.

Attack

  • Move to a square next to your opponent to attack them.
  • Once there, click on your opponent to hit them.
  • Can you find anything lying around that will do more damage than the cutlery you are currently holding?
